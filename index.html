Welcome

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Environmental Science and New Energy Technology at Tsinghua-Berkeley Shenzhen Institute (TBSI), advised by Prof. Junqiao Wu and Prof. Feiyu Kang.

Research

My research interests focus on the multivalent energy storage devices especially aqueous rechargeable zinc ion battery (ARZIB). I am also interested in machine learning based materials design and discovery.

Multivalent Energy Storage Devices

Multivalent ions are utilized as the working ions for energy storage, which could significantly improve the energy density of the energy storage devices.

Machine Learning based Materials Design and Discovery

Ultilize machine learning and deep learning techniques to make materials design and discovery
